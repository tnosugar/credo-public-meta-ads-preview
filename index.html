Crēdo Legal · Meta Ads Phase 1 Preview

Six verticals × five deliverables (24 statics + 6 videos). Watercolor "After the Letter" direction. Copy structured to Context → Pain → Solution → Outcome.

Copy framework. Every primary text walks the reader through four beats: where you are now (Context), what hurts (Pain), what we do (Solution: our attorneys plus a concrete mechanism), what could change (Outcome: a possible after-state, framed with "could" to avoid overpromising).
Interactive prototype. Click any thumbnail below an ad to swap that format into the live mockup. The 9:16 video plays inline with native controls. Long primary text is auto-truncated at 3 lines with a "See more" link, matching real Facebook behavior.
